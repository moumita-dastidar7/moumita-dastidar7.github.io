About Easy Cook

Whether you’re a complete beginner or just looking for quick meal ideas, Easy Cook is here to help you enjoy the process of creating delicious meals.

From one-pan dinners to 10-minute snacks, Easy Cook is designed for people with limited time, budget, or experience. Join our community and start your journey toward becoming a confident home cook.

Easy Pasta

A quick 15-minute pasta recipe perfect for beginners. Toss with olive oil, garlic, and parmesan for a classic taste.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked pasta and toss well.
  4. Mix in grated parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

One-Pan Chicken

Minimal effort, maximum flavor. Cook chicken, veggies, and spices together in one pan for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place chicken and vegetables on a baking tray.
  3. Season with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Veggie Stir-Fry

A colorful mix of vegetables sautéed in a savory sauce. Serve with rice or noodles for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Rice or noodles to serve

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, then add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce and stir well.
  3. Serve over rice or noodles and enjoy!

Banana Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes made with ripe bananas and a hint of cinnamon. Perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Mash the bananas and mix with egg, flour, cinnamon, and milk.
  2. Heat a pan and pour batter to form small pancakes.
  3. Cook on both sides until golden brown.
  4. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy!

Avocado Toast

Creamy avocado spread over toasted bread, topped with chili flakes, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices bread
  • Chili flakes
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it over the toast.
  3. Sprinkle with chili flakes and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Chili Con Carne

A hearty and spicy dish made with ground beef, beans, tomatoes, and spices. Serve with rice or tortilla chips.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a pan, then add beans and tomatoes.
  2. Season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice or tortilla chips and enjoy!

Smoothie Bowl

Blend fruits and yogurt into a thick smoothie, topped with granola, seeds, and fresh fruit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Granola
  • Chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Blend the banana, mixed berries, and yogurt until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and chia seeds.
  3. Serve and enjoy!

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Golden and crispy on the outside, gooey and cheesy on the inside. A timeless comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt butter on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place a slice of cheese between the two slices of bread with the buttered sides facing out.
  3. Grill the sandwich until golden and crispy on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Shakshuka

Eggs poached in a spiced tomato and bell pepper sauce. Best served with crusty bread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, sauté the onion and bell pepper in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. Create two wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each one.
  4. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the eggs are poached to your liking.
  5. Serve with crusty bread and enjoy!

Quick Fried Rice

A simple stir-fry with rice, veggies, and your choice of protein. A great way to use up leftovers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, then add veggies and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Push the veggies to one side and scramble the egg on the other side of the pan.
  3. Add the rice, soy sauce, and cooked chicken (if using), and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Cooking Tips

Tip 1

Prep all your ingredients before starting to cook – it saves time and reduces stress.

Tip 2

Use fresh herbs and spices to elevate the flavor of any dish.

Tip 3

Invest in a good-quality knife – it makes prep work much easier.

Tip 4

Clean as you go to keep your workspace organized and tidy.

Tip 5

Taste your food as you cook to adjust seasoning and ensure it’s perfect.

Tip 6

Keep your pantry stocked with basics like olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Tip 7

Share your cooking experience on social media! We aim to have at least 20% of our visitors try a recipe and share their experience, helping to create a community of home cooks who inspire one another.
